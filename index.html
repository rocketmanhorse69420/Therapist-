About Me

I'm Grace Miller, LPCA, a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate with a Master's in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. My approach combines cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness, and trauma-informed care to support each client's unique journey.

I believe in building trust through empathy, active listening, and collaboration. Your story matters, and I'm here to guide you toward your goals.
